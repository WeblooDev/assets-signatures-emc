Arlington ISD TSI Readiness Dashboard

Before spring break Mrs. Traci Thomas-Braggs, Director of Postsecondary Partnerships for Arlington ISD requested a new dashboard for TSI. She needed a way to show a district and campus level view of TSI Progress Status that would update automatically as more TSIA/SAT/ACT scores are added. The TSI Readiness Dashboard sorts the students into Met, Met in ELAR only, Met in Math only, or not met in either. This solution is a game-changer for identifying students who are on the cusp of meeting TSI requirements. By leveraging real-time data, Arlington ISD will be able to provide targeted support to those students who need it most, ensuring they are fully prepared for college and career success. We’re excited to see the impact this will have on student outcomes and look forward to continuing our collaboration to drive success!

CC Solutions Network Spring Strategy Session

Please save the date, April 9th! Our CC Solutions Network Spring Strategy Session is designed to provide K‑12 districts with targeted, actionable insights into how they can leverage the CC Solutions platform to improve College, Career, and Military Readiness (CCMR) outcomes for their students. These sessions focus on specific areas of need and empower district leaders, counselors, and administrators to use data effectively in their decision‑making process. Our Spring Strategy session will focus on Career and Technical Education (CTE) and the powerful impact of leveraging the Program of Study dashboard to improve CCMR outcomes. We will showcase districts leading the way in using data to enhance career readiness, and we are eager to learn from their experiences.
